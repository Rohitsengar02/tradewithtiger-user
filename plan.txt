# TradeWithTiger - Course Selling App Plan

## 1. App Vision
A premium, high-performance educational platform designed for seamless learning with a world-class UI/UX. Focused on "TradeWithTiger" branding, featuring dark-mode aesthetics, gold/neon gradients, and fluid animations.

## 2. Core Features
- **Smart Dashboard**: Personalized course recommendations and "Daily Trading Insights" carousel.
- **Micro-Learning**: Bite-sized video modules optimized for mobile viewing.
- **Offline Mode**: Encrypted downloads for lessons and resources.
- **Gamification**: Badges, streaks, and leaderboards to keep learners engaged.
- **Community Hub**: In-app forums and peer-to-peer chat for collaborative learning.
- **Secure Payments**: Integration with Stripe/Razorpay/RevenueCat for one-time and subscription models.
- **Live Sessions**: Integration for live trading webinars or Q&A sessions.
- **AI Tutor**: Instant AI-powered assistance for course-related queries.
- **Certificates**: Automatically generated, blockchain-verified completion certificates.

## 3. App Pages
### Onboarding & Auth
- **Dynamic Splash Screen**: Logo animation using Rive/Lottie.
- **Interactive Onboarding**: 3-step carousel explaining app value.
- **Auth Flow**: Phone OTP, Google/Apple Login with sleek glassmorphism forms.

### Browse & Discover
- **Home**: Carousel of featured courses, trending categories, and "Top Mentors".
- **Search & Filter**: Real-time search with chips for sub-niches (e.g., Forex, Crypto, Stocks).
- **Course Landing Page**: Video trailers, syllabus accordion, mentor bio, and student reviews.

### Learning Experience
- **Premium Video Player**: Gesture controls, speed adjustment, PIP mode, and landscape lock.
- **Resource Center**: View and download PDFs, templates, and spreadsheets.
- **Quizzes & Tests**: Animated interactive quizzes at the end of modules.

### Social & Profile
- **Global Leaderboard**: Gamified ranking of top learners.
- **User Profile**: Wallet for tiger-points, earned certificates, and transaction history.
- **Settings**: Theme switcher (Dark/Light), Notification management.

## 4. UI/UX Design System
- **Typography**: `Outfit` or `Inter` for a modern, clean look.
- **Palette**: Dark Charcoal (#121212), Gold (#FFD700), Neon Green (#39FF14) for trading accents.
- **Animations**: `flutter_animate` for scroll effects, `lottie` for state changes (success/fail).
- **Icons**: `hugeicons` or `font_awesome_flutter` for a consistent premium feel.

## 5. Recommended Tech Stack (Flutter)
- **State Management**: `Riverpod` or `Bloc` (Industry standard).
- **Networking**: `Dio` with interceptors for auth tokens.
- **Storage**: `Isar` or `Hive` (Fast local database).
- **Animations**: `flutter_animate`, `lottie`, `rive`.
- **UI Components**: `carousel_slider`, `smooth_page_indicator`, `shimmer`.
- **Payments**: `flutter_stripe` or `razorpay_flutter`.
